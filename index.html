Hon. Eric Edem Agbana is a dynamic Ghanaian politician and social entrepreneur dedicated to youth empowerment, governance, and community development.
He holds a strong academic background, with two master's degrees in Economic Policy Management and Development Finance. He is currently pursuing a PhD in Leadership and Governance.
As a former National Deputy Youth Organizer of the National Democratic Congress (NDC), Hon. Agbana has been instrumental in advocating for policies that support education, job creation, and national development.
His leadership extends beyond politics—he has spearheaded numerous initiatives focused on youth and community development, demonstrating a deep commitment to social progress and good governance.
